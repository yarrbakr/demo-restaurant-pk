Our Story

A legacy of flavour, passed down through generations.

From a Family Kitchen to Faisalabad's Favourite

In 1995, with a dream and a handful of cherished family recipes, the doors of Zaika Restaurant opened on Main Susan Road, Faisalabad. What began as a modest eatery quickly became a beloved gathering place for families, friends, and food lovers across the city.

Our founder believed in a simple philosophy: use the freshest ingredients, honour traditional cooking methods, and serve every guest as if they were family. That belief still guides everything we do today.

Over the decades, we have grown from a small family kitchen into one of Faisalabad's most trusted names in Pakistani cuisine. Yet we remain true to our roots — every dish is prepared with the same care and passion that started it all.
