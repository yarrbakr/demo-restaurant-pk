A Taste of Tradition Since 1995

Welcome to Zaika Restaurant, where every dish tells the story of Pakistan's rich culinary heritage. Nestled in the heart of Faisalabad on Main Susan Road, we have been serving authentic Pakistani cuisine for nearly three decades.

Our recipes have been passed down through generations, preserving the original flavours that make Pakistani food truly extraordinary. From our signature Karahi to slow-cooked Nihari, every bite is a celebration of tradition.
